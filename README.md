# Quizzar
Quizzar, a hybrid term coined out of the juxtaposition of ‘Quasar’ and ‘Quizzer’, is a Sci-Fi quiz.
It is conducted by the Astronomy Club of BITS Pilani, Pilani Campus during the cultural fest "Qasis".
The quiz consist of questions that shall embody scientific concepts that have been portrayed in Movies, TV series, Anime, Manga, Books and other pop-culture references.
